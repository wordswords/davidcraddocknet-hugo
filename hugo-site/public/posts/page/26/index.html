Converting week numbers to dates

Here is some python code I adapted from this stackoverflow post to get the first day of a week specificed by a week number. This method includes leap year and summer time differences. 1 2 3 4 import time def weeknum(num,year): instr = str(year)+" "+str(num-1)+" 1" print time.asctime(time.strptime(instr,'%Y %W %w')) Here is me exectuting the code in Python’s IDLE shell: See that the first week of 2009 actually started in 2008, but by the end of that week we are in 2009....

MediaMonkey allows you to transfer music from any computer onto your guest iPhone

Normally only one iTunes install can be associated with your iPhone, but MediaMonkey allows you another way to transfer music and audio files with a ‘guest’ iPhone.
